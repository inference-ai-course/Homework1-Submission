## Executive Summary

**Key Updates & Announcements:**

- **Visa Bond Requirements:** As of October 23, 2025, specific countries are subject to visa bond requirements, affecting visa issuance procedures for nationals of designated countries.

- **Country of Residence Processing Policy:** New policies implemented on October 10, 2025, require both immigrant and nonimmigrant visa applicants to be adjudicated in their country of residence, impacting international travel and visa processing procedures.

- **Employment-Based Immigration Limits:** Multiple employment-based visa categories have reached their annual limits in 2025, including EB-1, EB-2, EB-3, EB-4, and EB-5 categories, significantly affecting immigrant workers and their employers.

- **Diversity Visa Program Updates:** The DV-2025 program has reached the 7% cap for Egypt, and DV-2026 program information has been updated with corrections to the Federal Register notice.

- **Interview Waiver Extensions:** The Department of State has extended interview waiver eligibility for certain nonimmigrant visa categories through September 18, 2025, streamlining the application process for eligible applicants.
